Twiceland - The Opening (2017)

Twice 1st Tour: Twiceland - The Opening on February 17, 2017 and ends on June 18, 2017.

Their 1st tour lasting three days in Seoul, one day in Bangkok, one day in Singapore, and two days in Seoul for the Encore with a total attendees of 36,000.
